As industrial applications expand alongside smart city initiatives and connected healthcare systems, the demand for IoT Edge Gateways is surging.</p><p>The IoT Edge Gateways Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.8% during the forecast period, driven by increased adoption across various sectors such as manufacturing, transportation, and utilities. Additionally, there is a rising emphasis on cybersecurity solutions to protect sensitive data processed at the edge.
