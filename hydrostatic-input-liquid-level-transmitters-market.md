<p><ul><li>Rod Level Transmitter</li><li>Cable level transmitter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hydrostatic input liquid level transmitters measure the pressure exerted by a liquid column to determine its level. The rod level transmitter type features a rigid rod submerged in the liquid, providing accurate readings over varying depths. In contrast, the cable level transmitter utilizes a flexible cable that can freely move within the liquid, ideal for applications with limited space or varying tank shapes. Both types are essential in industries like petrochemical, water treatment, and food processing for reliable level monitoring.</p></p>
