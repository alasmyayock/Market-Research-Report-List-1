<p><ul><li>Point Level Probes</li><li>Continuous Level Probes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Level probes are essential instruments used to measure fluid levels in various applications. The market is primarily divided into two types: point level probes and continuous level probes. Point level probes detect the presence or absence of a substance at a specific point, making them suitable for applications like overflow protection. Continuous level probes, however, provide real-time monitoring of fluid levels throughout a range, enabling more dynamic control in processes such as tanks and reservoirs. Each type serves unique operational needs across diverse industries.</p></p>
