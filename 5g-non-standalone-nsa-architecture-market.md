<p><p>5G Non-Standalone (NSA) Architecture is an approach that allows mobile network operators to utilize existing 4G LTE infrastructure alongside new 5G technology. This hybrid model enables operators to offer enhanced services without requiring a complete overhaul of their existing networks. The NSA architecture primarily focuses on leveraging LTE for control signaling while delivering higher data rates using 5G radio technology. This enables quicker deployment of 5G services, reducing the time and cost associated with transitioning to a fully standalone network.</p><p>The 5G Non-Standalone (NSA) Architecture Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.9% during the forecast period.
