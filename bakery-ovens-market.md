<p><p>The bakery ovens market primarily includes convection ovens and deck ovens. Convection ovens utilize fans to circulate hot air, ensuring even baking and reducing cooking time, making them ideal for pastries and bread. Deck ovens, on the other hand, feature stone or ceramic baking surfaces that provide optimal heat retention and moisture control, perfect for artisan bread and pizzas. Both types cater to different baking needs, helping bakeries achieve a variety of textures and flavors in their products.</p></p>
