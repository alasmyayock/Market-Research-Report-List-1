<p><p>The bending tool market encompasses three main types: manual, electric, and hydraulic. Manual bending tools rely on human effort, providing simplicity and portability but requiring more physical strength and skill. Electric bending tools offer enhanced efficiency and consistency, making them suitable for moderate to heavy tasks with less physical strain. Hydraulic bending tools utilize pressurized fluid for powerful bending capabilities, ideal for heavy-duty applications, offering speed and precision for complex shapes in industrial settings. Each type serves distinct user needs and preferences.</p></p>
