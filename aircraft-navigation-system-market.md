<p><strong>The Aircraft Navigation System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Instrument Navigation System</li><li>Radio Navigation System</li><li>Inertial Navigation System</li><li>Integrated Navigation System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft navigation system market comprises four key types: </p><p>1. **Instrument Navigation System**: Utilizes cockpit instruments to determine position and orientation.  </p><p>2. **Radio Navigation System**: Employs ground-based radio signals for positioning and guidance.  </p><p>3. **Inertial Navigation System**: Relies on onboard sensors to calculate position based on movement without external references.  </p><p>4. **Integrated Navigation System**: Combines multiple navigation methods for improved accuracy and reliability, enhancing overall aircraft efficiency and safety.</p></p>
