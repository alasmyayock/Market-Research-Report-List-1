<p><p>The Mandarin essential oil market consists of three main types: absolutes, concentrates, and blends. Absolutes are highly concentrated oils extracted through solvents, offering a rich aroma suitable for high-end applications. Concentrates are distilled oils that retain intense fragrance and therapeutic properties, ideal for aromatherapy and personal care products. Blends combine Mandarin oil with other essential oils or carriers, enhancing versatility in applications such as cosmetics and fragrances. Together, these types cater to diverse consumer preferences and market demands.</p></p>
