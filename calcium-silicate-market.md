<p><p>Calcium silicate is categorized into three types based on density: low density, medium density, and high density. Low-density calcium silicate is lightweight and primarily used for insulation in construction, offering thermal resistance. Medium-density variants provide a balance between strength and insulation, suitable for applications in industrial settings. High-density calcium silicate is robust, often used in fireproofing and structural applications where durability and resistance to high temperatures are crucial. Each type serves specific functions across various industries.</p></p>
