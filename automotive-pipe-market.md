<p><p>The automotive pipe market is primarily segmented into welded and seamless pipe types. Welded pipes are formed by joining metal sheets through welding, offering cost efficiency and versatility in various applications. They are commonly used in non-structural components. Seamless pipes, on the other hand, are produced without any seams or joints, enhancing strength and durability, making them ideal for high-pressure applications. Both types are essential in automotive manufacturing, addressing diverse performance and safety requirements.</p></p>
