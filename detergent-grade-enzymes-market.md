<p><p>The detergent-grade enzymes market includes key types such as protease, amylase, lipase, and cellulase. Protease breaks down proteins, effectively removing stains from fabrics. Amylase targets starch-based stains, enhancing cleaning efficiency in laundry and dishwashing detergents. Lipase acts on fat molecules, while cellulase improves fabric care and brightness by breaking down cellulose in fibers. Together, these enzymes boost the effectiveness of detergents, catering to diverse cleaning needs and promoting eco-friendly cleaning solutions in household and industrial applications.</p></p>
