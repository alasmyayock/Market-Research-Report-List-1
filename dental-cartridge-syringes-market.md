<p><p>The dental cartridge syringes market comprises two main types: aspirating and non-aspirating syringes. Aspirating syringes are designed to allow dentists to detect the presence of blood during injection, ensuring that the needle is not placed in a blood vessel, thus enhancing patient safety. Non-aspirating syringes, on the other hand, are simpler and do not have this feature, making them easier to use for straightforward dental procedures. Both types cater to varying clinical needs in dental practices.</p></p>
