<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The NICU Catheters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Peripherally Inserted Central Catheters [PICCs]</li><li>Central Venous Catheters [CVCs]</li><li>Umbilical Venous Catheters [UVCs]</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The NICU catheters market includes several types of catheters designed for neonatal care. Peripherally Inserted Central Catheters (PICCs) are long, flexible tubes inserted in peripheral veins to access central circulation. Central Venous Catheters (CVCs) provide direct access to a central vein for medication and nutrition. Umbilical Venous Catheters (UVCs) are placed in the umbilical vein of newborns for immediate access to the circulatory system. The "Others" category encompasses additional specialized catheters tailored for specific clinical needs in neonatology.</p></p>
