<p><strong>The Dialysis Catheters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Long-Term Hemodialysis Catheters</li><li>Short-Term Hemodialysis Catheters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dialysis catheters market is categorized into long-term and short-term hemodialysis catheters. Long-term hemodialysis catheters are designed for extended use, typically placed for several weeks to months, facilitating ongoing dialysis treatment in patients with chronic kidney failure. In contrast, short-term hemodialysis catheters are intended for temporary access, usually used for a few days to weeks, often during acute kidney injury or while waiting for a more permanent solution. Both types ensure efficient blood access for dialysis procedures.</p></p>
