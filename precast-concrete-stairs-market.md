<p><p>The precast concrete stairs market includes beam-type stairs and slab stairs. Beam-type stairs consist of precast concrete beams with treads and risers, offering structural support and design flexibility. They are ideal for heavy-duty applications and long spans. Slab stairs, on the other hand, are monolithic precast units with integrated treads and risers, providing a sleek, uniform appearance. They are lightweight, easy to install, and suitable for residential and commercial projects. Both types are durable, cost-effective, and customizable, catering to diverse construction needs.</p></p>
