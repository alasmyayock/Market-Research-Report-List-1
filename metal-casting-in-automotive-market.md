<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Metal Casting in Automotive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gravity Casting</li><li>High-Pressure Die Casting (HPDC)</li><li>Low-Pressure Die Casting (LPDC)</li><li>Sand Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal casting in the automotive market encompasses various techniques, each serving specific applications. Gravity casting utilizes gravity to pour molten metal into molds, ideal for large parts. High-pressure die casting (HPDC) allows rapid production of intricate components with excellent surface finishes, making it suitable for high-volume applications. Low-pressure die casting (LPDC) offers enhanced control and reduced defects for complex shapes. Sand casting, a versatile and cost-effective method, uses sand molds, accommodating diverse part sizes but typically with a rougher finish. Each method caters to different automotive design needs.</p></p>
