<p><p>The automatic pool covers market is primarily divided into hydraulic and electric systems. Hydraulic covers operate using fluid pressure to open and close the cover, providing powerful and quiet operation, often suitable for larger pools. Electric covers, on the other hand, utilize electric motors for movement, offering a more straightforward installation and maintenance process. Both types enhance pool safety and energy efficiency, catering to different user preferences and pool sizes, ensuring convenience and ease of use.</p></p>
