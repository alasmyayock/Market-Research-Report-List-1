<p><p>The solid white film market is segmented into heat sealable and non-heat sealable types. Heat sealable solid white films are designed to bond securely when exposed to heat, making them ideal for packaging applications that require airtight seals, such as food and pharmaceuticals. Non-heat sealable solid white films, on the other hand, do not require heat for sealing, offering versatility for applications where adhesive or mechanical fastening is preferred. Both types cater to varying industry needs based on functionality and performance.</p></p>
