<p><p>The hot water solutions market can be categorized into centralized and decentralized systems. Centralized systems involve a single, large water heating unit that supplies hot water to multiple outlets, making it efficient for larger buildings or facilities. In contrast, decentralized systems utilize smaller, localized units that provide hot water directly at the point of use, offering flexibility and reduced energy loss. Each type addresses varying needs in terms of capacity, efficiency, and installation costs, catering to different consumer preferences and requirements.</p></p>
