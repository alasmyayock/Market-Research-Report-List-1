<p><p>The semi-chemical wood pulp market consists of various types based on pulping methods. The Neutral Sulfite Method utilizes neutral pH sulfite for efficient delignification. The Bisulfite Method employs bisulfite solutions, favoring wood fibers with high yield. The Green Liquor Pulping Method uses green liquor for a more sustainable process. The Sulfur-Free Pulping Method eliminates sulfur compounds, focusing on eco-friendliness. Lastly, the Alkaline Sulfite Method combines alkali and sulfite for effective pulp production, balancing quality and environmental impact.</p></p>
