<p><p>Ultrasonic baths are cleaning devices that use high-frequency sound waves to create microscopic bubbles in a liquid cleaning solution. This process, known as cavitation, gently agitates the solution, allowing it to penetrate and dislodge contaminants from surfaces, even in hard-to-reach areas. Ultrasonic baths are widely used in sectors such as healthcare, automotive, jewelry, and electronics for their effectiveness in cleaning intricate items like surgical instruments, delicate jewelry, and electronic components.</p><p>The Ultrasonic Baths Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.1% during the forecast period.
