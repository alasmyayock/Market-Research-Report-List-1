<p><ul><li>Line Resist</li><li>Space Resist</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ArF implant resists market is divided into two primary types: line resist and space resist. Line resist is designed for patterning dense features, ensuring sharp and well-defined edges, ideal for high-resolution applications. In contrast, space resist is tailored for creating gaps or spaces between features, aiding in the formation of wider patterns. Both types play critical roles in photolithography processes, enabling the fabrication of advanced semiconductor devices while maintaining accuracy and fidelity in feature size and spacing.</p></p>
