<p><p>The eRx (Electronic Prescription) System market is categorized into two main types: on-premise solutions and cloud-based solutions. On-premise solutions involve installing software directly on a pharmacy's local servers, providing greater control and security but requiring significant IT resources for maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based solutions offer a subscription model hosted on remote servers, allowing for easier updates, scalability, and accessibility from various devices. Both types aim to enhance prescription accuracy, streamline workflows, and improve patient safety in healthcare settings.</p></p>
