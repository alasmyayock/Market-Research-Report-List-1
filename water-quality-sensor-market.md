<p><p>The water quality sensor market encompasses various types including chlorine residual sensors, TOC sensors, turbidity sensors, conductivity sensors, pH sensors, and ORP sensors. Chlorine residual sensors measure chlorine levels for disinfection control. TOC sensors assess total organic carbon in water, indicating organic pollutant levels. Turbidity sensors evaluate water clarity by measuring suspended solids. Conductivity sensors detect ion concentration, while pH sensors measure acidity or alkalinity. ORP sensors gauge the oxidation-reduction potential, indicating water's chemical state and stability.</p></p>
