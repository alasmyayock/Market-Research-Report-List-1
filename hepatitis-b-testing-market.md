<p><p>The Hepatitis B testing market is categorized into three main types: virus detection, antibody testing, and antigen testing. Virus detection identifies the presence of the Hepatitis B virus (HBV) in the blood, crucial for diagnosing active infections. Antibody testing looks for antibodies produced in response to the virus, helping to determine past infections or vaccination status. Antigen testing detects specific viral proteins, indicating an active infection. Together, these testing types facilitate effective screening, diagnosis, and monitoring of Hepatitis B.</p></p>
