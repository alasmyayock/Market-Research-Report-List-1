<p><p>The Asphalt Rubber Binder market primarily consists of two types: Modified Asphalt and Impregnated Asphalt. Modified Asphalt involves blending traditional asphalt with rubber from tires, enhancing flexibility and resistance to deformation, thus improving durability and performance. Impregnated Asphalt, on the other hand, incorporates rubber into the asphalt matrix, allowing for increased adhesion and water resistance. Both types contribute to sustainable road construction by utilizing recycled materials, improving pavement longevity, and reducing maintenance costs in the road network.</p></p>
