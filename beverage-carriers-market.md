<p><p>Beverage carriers are categorized into two main market types: semi-rigid and rigid. Semi-rigid carriers offer flexibility and can conform to the shape of the beverages they hold, making them suitable for various container types like cans and bottles. Rigid carriers, on the other hand, are designed with a fixed structure, providing robust support and durability while ensuring stability during transport. Both types cater to different needs in packaging, storage, and distribution within the beverage industry.</p></p>
