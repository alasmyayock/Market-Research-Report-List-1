<p><p>The water recycling system market encompasses various technologies for purifying and reusing water. Reverse osmosis systems remove contaminants by forcing water through a semipermeable membrane. Distillation systems vaporize water to separate impurities, utilizing heat for purification. Disinfection methods, such as UV treatment or chemical dosing, eliminate pathogens to ensure water safety. Filtration methods, including sand or activated carbon filters, physically remove particles and pollutants. Each technology serves to enhance water sustainability and quality across various applications, from industrial to residential use.</p></p>
