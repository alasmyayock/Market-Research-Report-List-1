<p><strong>The Firestop Sealants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Elastomeric Type</li><li>Intumescent Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The firestop sealants market primarily consists of two types: elastomeric and intumescent sealants. Elastomeric sealants are flexible materials that provide a waterproof and durable barrier, allowing for movement in building materials while preventing fire and smoke passage. Intumescent sealants, on the other hand, expand when exposed to high temperatures, forming a char that blocks heat and flames, thus enhancing fire resistance. Both types are essential for ensuring the safety of structures by protecting against fire hazards.</p></p>
