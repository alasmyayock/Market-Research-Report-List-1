<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Skin Graft Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Autologous</li><li>Isogeneic</li><li>Allogeneic</li><li>Xenogeneic</li><li>Prosthetic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The skin graft market comprises several types: </p><p>- **Autologous grafts** use the patient’s own skin, minimizing rejection risks.  </p><p>- **Isogeneic grafts** are taken from genetically identical individuals, often twin donors.  </p><p>- **Allogeneic grafts** come from different individuals, requiring careful matching to prevent rejection.  </p><p>- **Xenogeneic grafts** utilize skin from different species, facing immunological challenges.  </p><p>- **Prosthetic options** replace lost skin with synthetic materials, providing versatile solutions where natural grafts are impractical. Each type serves distinct clinical needs based on patient conditions.</p></p>
