Picea mariana, known as black spruce, is favored for its lightweight and excellent acoustic properties. Picea engelmannii offers high strength and is commonly used in musical instruments. Picea sitchensis, or Sitka spruce, is prized for its superior performance in marine applications. Picea rubens is used in general construction. Picea orientalis is often chosen for ornamental purposes, while Picea abies is popular in Europe for its versatility. Picea glauca and Picea pungens are appreciated for their stability and are sought after for cabinetry and decorative uses.</p></p>
