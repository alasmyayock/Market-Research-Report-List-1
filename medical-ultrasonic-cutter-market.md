<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Non-contact</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical ultrasonic cutter market is divided into two main types: contact and non-contact cutters. Contact ultrasonic cutters utilize direct physical contact with the tissue to facilitate precise cutting and coagulation, making them ideal for surgical applications. In contrast, non-contact ultrasonic cutters employ ultrasonic vibrations without direct tissue contact, allowing for minimal thermal damage and enhanced safety. Both types serve distinct surgical needs, offering various benefits in terms of precision, safety, and efficacy in medical procedures.</p></p>
