Ethoxylated fatty acid amines are surfactants that reduce static electricity in plastics and textiles. Diethanolamides serve as emulsifiers and also mitigate static in various formulations. Glycerol monostearate is a non-ionic emulsifier that provides anti-static properties, particularly in food and cosmetic applications. Together, these agents enhance product performance by minimizing static cling and improving processing efficiency across multiple industries.</p></p>
