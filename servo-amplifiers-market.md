<p><p>The servo amplifiers market is primarily divided into two types: DC servo amplifiers and AC servo amplifiers. DC servo amplifiers use direct current to control the motor's position and speed, offering high precision and responsiveness, ideal for applications like robotics and CNC machinery. On the other hand, AC servo amplifiers utilize alternating current, providing higher efficiency and smoother operation, making them suitable for complex motion control tasks in industries such as manufacturing, aerospace, and automotive automation.</p></p>
