<p><p>The bread baking enzyme market comprises various enzyme types that enhance baking processes. Protease enzymes improve dough elasticity and reduce mixing time, leading to better texture. Carbohydrase enzymes, including amylases, break down starches into fermentable sugars, enhancing fermentation and dough rise. Lipase enzymes contribute to improving dough extensibility and flavor by breaking down fats. Together, these enzymes optimize bread quality, promote consistency, and reduce production costs, making them essential in modern baking practices.</p></p>
