<p><strong>The Electronic Medical Records (EMR) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Client Server Setups</li><li>Cloud-based Setups</li><li>Hybrid Setups</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electronic Medical Records (EMR) market comprises three main types: client-server setups, cloud-based setups, and hybrid setups. Client-server setups involve locally hosted servers that store patient data, offering robust control and security but requiring significant IT infrastructure. Cloud-based setups leverage internet-based services for storage and access, promoting ease of use, scalability, and reduced maintenance costs. Hybrid setups combine elements of both, allowing for localized data management alongside cloud storage, providing flexibility and optimizing resource utilization for healthcare providers.</p></p>
