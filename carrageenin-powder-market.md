<p><p>The carrageenan powder market consists of three main types: Kappa, Iota, and Lambda. Kappa carrageenan, derived from red seaweed, is commonly used as a gelling agent in food products due to its strong gel-forming properties. Iota carrageenan offers a softer gel and is utilized in dairy applications. Lambda carrageenan, known for its thickening abilities, does not gel effectively but enhances texture in various formulations. Each type serves distinct functions in the food, pharmaceutical, and cosmetic industries.</p></p>
