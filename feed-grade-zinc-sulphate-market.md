<p><p>The feed grade zinc sulfate market is primarily divided into two types: Zinc Sulfate Heptahydrate and Zinc Sulfate Monohydrate. Zinc Sulfate Heptahydrate, containing seven water molecules, is widely used in animal nutrition for its high solubility and bioavailability, promoting growth and immunity in livestock. Zinc Sulfate Monohydrate, with one water molecule, is favored for its lower moisture content and longer shelf life. Both forms play crucial roles in supplementing zinc levels in animal feed, supporting overall health and productivity.</p></p>
