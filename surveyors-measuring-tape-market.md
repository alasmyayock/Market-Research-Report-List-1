<p><p>Surveyors measuring tapes are categorized into three main types: hook end, ring end, and zero end. Hook end tapes feature a metal hook that allows for easy attachment to surfaces, ensuring accurate measurements. Ring end tapes have a loop that can be anchored or attached for stability during measurement. Zero end tapes are designed with a precise starting point at the end of the tape, eliminating any discrepancies in measurement from the hook or ring, providing accuracy in surveying tasks.</p></p>
