<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Pitch System</li><li>Electrical Pitch System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wind turbine pitch system market primarily consists of hydraulic and electrical pitch systems. Hydraulic pitch systems use fluid pressure to adjust the blade angle, offering robust performance, especially in extreme conditions, but may require more maintenance. In contrast, electrical pitch systems utilize electric motors and sensors for more precise control of blade positioning, focused on efficiency and responsiveness. Both systems play a crucial role in optimizing energy capture and enhancing turbine performance, catering to different operational needs and project requirements.</p></p>
