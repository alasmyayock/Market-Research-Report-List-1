<p><p>The dual power switches market encompasses three key types: CT (Current Transformer), PET (Power Electronic Transformer), and SPECT (Smart Power Electronic Transformer). CT switches are primarily used for efficient power distribution and monitoring, ensuring reliability in energy systems. PET switches enhance grid management through seamless integration of renewable energy sources. SPECT combines advanced digital technology with power electronics, enabling smart grid functionalities and real-time energy management. Together, these systems facilitate a more resilient and efficient energy infrastructure.</p></p>
