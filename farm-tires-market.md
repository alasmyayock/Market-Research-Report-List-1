<p><p>The farm tires market is primarily segmented into bias tires and radial tires. Bias tires feature crisscrossed fabric layers, offering superior durability and a robust grip ideal for rugged terrain, making them suitable for various agricultural applications. In contrast, radial tires have a flexible sidewall and belts that run perpendicular to the tread, providing enhanced traction, better fuel efficiency, and improved ride comfort. Both types cater to diverse farming needs, influencing their adoption based on specific operational requirements.</p></p>
