<p><p>The MEMS lens market features various types based on actuation mechanisms. The electromagnetic-driven type utilizes magnetic fields to precisely control lens positioning, offering fast response times and accuracy. The piezoelectric-driven type employs piezoelectric materials that deform under electric voltage, enabling rapid adjustments and fine-tuning of optical properties. Additional market types may include capacitive or thermal-driven mechanisms, each catering to specific applications in consumer electronics, automotive, and medical devices, enhancing performance and enabling compact designs in advanced optical systems.</p></p>
