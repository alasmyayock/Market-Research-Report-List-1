<p><p>Sulfuryl fluoride (SO₂F₂) is a colorless, odorless gas widely used as a fumigant for pest control, particularly in the agricultural and construction industries. It is effective against a broad range of pests, including termites, beetles, and rodents, making it a preferred choice for structural fumigation and post-harvest treatment of stored commodities. Its non-flammable and non-corrosive properties further enhance its utility in various applications.</p><p>The Sulfuryl Fluoride Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.3% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by increasing demand for pest control solutions in agriculture, rising awareness about food safety, and stringent regulations regarding the use of harmful chemicals. The construction sector's expansion, particularly in developing regions, is also contributing to market growth, as sulfuryl fluoride is extensively used for termite control in buildings.</p><p>Recent trends in the market include the development of eco-friendly fumigation methods and the adoption of sulfuryl fluoride as a replacement for methyl bromide, which is being phased out due to its ozone-depleting properties. Additionally, advancements in application technologies and the growing emphasis on integrated pest management practices are shaping the market landscape. However, concerns over its greenhouse gas potential and regulatory challenges may pose restraints to market expansion.</p></p>
