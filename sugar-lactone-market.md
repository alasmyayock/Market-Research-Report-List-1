<p><p>Sugar lactone, also known as glucono delta-lactone (GDL), is a naturally occurring food additive derived from glucose. It is widely used in the food and beverage industry as an acidulant, leavening agent, and preservative. Its mild sour taste and ability to regulate pH make it a popular ingredient in products like tofu, dairy, baked goods, and meat processing. Additionally, it is utilized in cosmetics and pharmaceuticals due to its stabilizing and moisturizing properties.</p><p>The Sugar Lactone Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.1% during the forecast period.
