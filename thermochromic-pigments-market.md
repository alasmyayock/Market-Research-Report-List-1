<p><strong>The Thermochromic Pigments Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reversible Thermochromic Pigment</li><li>Irreversible Thermochromic Pigment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermochromic pigments market is categorized into two main types: reversible and irreversible thermochromic pigments. Reversible thermochromic pigments change color with temperature variations but return to their original hue upon cooling, making them suitable for applications like mood-sensitive products and temperature indicators. Irreversible thermochromic pigments, on the other hand, undergo a permanent color change when exposed to heat, often used in security applications or to signal temperature thresholds. Both types find uses across industries, including textiles, packaging, and coatings.</p></p>
