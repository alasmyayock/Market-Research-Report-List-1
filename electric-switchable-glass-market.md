<p><p>Electric switchable glass, also known as smart glass, utilizes technologies like organic dyes, conducting polymers, and metal oxides to control light and heat transmission. Organic dyes change opacity when voltage is applied, offering energy efficiency and privacy. Conducting polymers enable rapid switching and durability, ideal for dynamic applications. Metal oxides, such as tungsten oxide, provide high transparency and stability, suitable for large-scale installations. Each type caters to diverse needs, from residential to commercial, balancing performance, cost, and environmental impact.</p></p>
