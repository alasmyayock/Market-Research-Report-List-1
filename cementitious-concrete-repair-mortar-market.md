<p><p>The cementitious concrete repair mortar market primarily comprises two types: Portland cement mortar and polymer modified cement mortar. Portland cement mortar is a traditional option that offers good strength and durability for general repairs. In contrast, polymer modified cement mortar incorporates polymers to enhance adhesion, flexibility, and resistance to cracking and water damage. This modification makes it suitable for more demanding applications, such as repairing structurally challenged concrete or areas exposed to harsh environmental conditions.</p></p>
