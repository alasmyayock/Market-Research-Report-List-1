<p><strong>The Chromatic Confocal Sensor Head Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Displacement Sensor Head</li><li>Thickness Sensor Head</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Chromatic Confocal Sensor Head market comprises two main types: displacement sensor heads and thickness sensor heads. Displacement sensor heads measure the distance between the sensor and the target surface, providing high-resolution data essential for precision applications. Thickness sensor heads, on the other hand, are designed to assess the thickness of transparent or reflective materials, ensuring accurate material characterization. Both types leverage chromatic confocal technology to deliver non-contact, real-time measurements, facilitating advancements in industrial automation and quality control processes.</p></p>
